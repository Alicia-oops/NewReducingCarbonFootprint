QUESTIONS TO ASK YOURSELF


Do you use solar, water, or air energy to generate cleaner electricity? Are you using energy efficient appliances? Are you heating or cooling your home efficiently? Do you try to travel less? Is your fuel efficient? Do you try to change up your mode of transportation every now and then? If the answer to most of these questions is no, consider becoming green
