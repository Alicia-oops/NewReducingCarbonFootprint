HOW DO I RECYCLE?


Starting to recycle can be a hard task to get used to at first, however if you start to create new habits now, in the end you will be able to become a recycling expert.


HOW DO I ORGANIZE MY RECYCLING?


When it comes to organizing recycling items, it can be troublesome not knowing what the recycling numbers of items might be. So, that's why we have included what the different types of numbers mean and how to recycle them properly. Each number within the recycling symbol is an actual id that helps trash collectors know how to process each type of material. That is why it is one of the crucial parts of recycling. Congratulations, you are going to become a recycling expert!
